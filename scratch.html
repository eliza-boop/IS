Fun Facts About Planets

  1. Largest Planet: Jupiter
    • Over 1,300 Earths could fit inside Jupiter.
    • It has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.
  2. Smallest Planet: Mercury
    • Mercury has no atmosphere to retain heat.
    • A day on Mercury is longer than a year.
